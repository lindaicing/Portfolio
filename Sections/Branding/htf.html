Background and Audience

The Hard Tech Fund is a startup accelerator and development program that assists UC Davis student projects that build hardware with environmental conservation in mind. The participating organizations are:

  • Bioinnovation Group at UC Davis
  • Blockchain at Davis
  • Hyperloop at UC Davis
  • Space and Satellite Club
  • Quantum Computing at UC Davis

My role in this project was creating the website layout, branding, logos, illustrations, flyers, and other visual marketing materials to promote the Hard Tech Fund, its associated projects, and its mission.
