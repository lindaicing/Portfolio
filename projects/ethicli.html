Overview

Ethicli suggests better products while you shop by scoring how the product affects our workers, planet, and community. Use our free browser extension to effortlessly shop from more ethical, green, and smaller businesses.

The site is coded using HTML/CSS and JavaScript(vanilla and JQuery). I coded the visual extension using HTML/CSS/JS, with my other teammates building the database in Java.
